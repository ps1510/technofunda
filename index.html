Methodology

How it Works

📡
Daily Data Collection
Every day after market close, our engine automatically downloads end-of-day price data for every stock in each country.
⚙️
RS Momentum Scoring
Each stock is scored on Relative Strength — how well it performs compared to its own sector and the overall market index.
🏆
Signal Classification
Stocks are classified from 🌟 Prime (Very Strong Bullish) through Confirmed and RS Bullish signals, down to 🔴 Bearish (breaking down vs market).
📊
Report Generation
Full interactive reports are built with sector rankings, top opportunities, chart patterns, and trade setups — all in one place.
🌐
Published Here
This page and all country reports are automatically published to the web every trading day — free, with no signup needed.
Understanding the Labels

What do the signals mean?

🌟 Prime
Very Strong Bullish
Strongest signal. Stock is outperforming on multiple timeframes with strong fundamentals. The best setups.
✅ Confirmed
Strong Bullish
Stock is outperforming the market and sector consistently. Good momentum, consider for watchlist.
📈 RS Leader
Bullish Relative Strength
Stock is showing positive RS vs market and sector. Early stage bullish momentum — watch for breakout.
👁 Watch
Neutral — Setup Building
Pre-conditions met but not yet confirmed. Stock is setting up — check back daily for progression.
⬜ Neutral
Neutral — No Signal
Mixed signals — stock is neither a clear leader nor laggard vs the market right now.
🔴 RS Breakdown
Bearish Relative Strength
Stock is significantly underperforming both its sector and the market. Caution advised.
